ModelMuse Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2025

Introduction

ModelMuse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

When you use ModelMuse, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and securely store your authentication information.
  • User Content: We store the AI prompts you create, including any metadata, tags, or descriptions you provide.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with the extension to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve ModelMuse
  • Store and sync your prompts across devices
  • Enable sharing functionality when you explicitly choose to share a prompt
  • Authenticate your account and protect against unauthorized access

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, our cloud database provider. All data is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that you can only access your own prompts unless you explicitly choose to share them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your prompts remain private unless you specifically choose to share them using the sharing feature within the extension.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data at any time through the Export feature
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Control which prompts are private or public

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@modelmuse.app
